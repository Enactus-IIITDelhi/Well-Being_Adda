Mental health and myths

Mental health is an intrinsic part of our well-being and influences our everyday lives.
We conducted an enriching session about mental health and the myths surrounding it on 16th February 2021. Mental health is an intrinsic part of our well-being and influences our everyday lives. Yet, it has remained a taboo and is a neglected topic with innumerable myths and misconceptions. The session shed light on these myths and taboos and how to be mentally healthy by overcoming these misconceptions.
