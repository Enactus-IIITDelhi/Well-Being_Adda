Well-Being Circle Sessions - SELF HUB Program

The Well-being Circle Sessions aimed to create a community and promote open conversations about emotional health and well-being.
Weekly sessions were held over a period of 7 weeks in March - April 2021 for students in classes 7th to 10th, to encourage conversation around common and relatable well-being topics such as peer pressure, bullying, procrastination, stress management, gratitude, perfectionism, and more. The sessions included video screenings and interactive activities that urged the students to introspect. The sessions resulted in enlightening discussions with interesting and creative inputs from the students and sharing of personal anecdotes.
