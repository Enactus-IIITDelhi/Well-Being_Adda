Mental health and stigma around it

Session on Mental Health and Stigma Around It in collaboration with Myndprime.
On 16th April 2021, we organised a Session on Mental Health and Stigma Around It in collaboration with Myndprime for students in classes 5th to 12th. The speaker for the session was Meraj Mir, a psychologist and psychotherapist from Myndprime. The session covered the topics of aspects of health, about mental health, mental health versus physical health, lingo of mental health, followed by Q&A session.
