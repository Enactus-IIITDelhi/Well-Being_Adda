#IAmThankfulFor

Upload an appreciate story for someone or something one is thankful to have in their life.
On 23rd August 2021 we had an instagram story challenge for our followers called #IAmThankfulFor. The Covid 19 pandemic was on a rise in India (from where our majority of followers are from) and we felt it was important to practice gratitude in these grim times.
We wanted our followers to remind themselves of all they have that makes their lives better but they may take it for granted. The challenge had people posting about all the beautiful things in the world from friends and family to pretty views and insightful literature. Well-Being Adda reshared all the stories to remind everyone of the good things in life. Around 35 stories were posted for the challenge.
