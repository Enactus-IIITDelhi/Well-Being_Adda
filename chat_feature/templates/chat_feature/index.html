Well-Being Adda

Well-being Adda aims to promote emotional health & holistic well-being.
Our initiatives include:

  • Targeting the stigma around mental health
  • Raising awareness about well-being
  • Creating solutions to make emotional support and therapy more accessible and affordable
  • Building an engaging and supportive community

Well-being Adda is a project taken up by Enactus IIITD and targets the 3rd UN SDG of Good Health and Well-being.

1 in 4 students suffer from emotional instability
India ranked 144/256 in UN's World Happiness Report
Only 28% Indians consider themselves happy
150mn people in India need care for their mental health condition
